Welcome to the BOB HR Training Decision Support System

This mini project was developed as part of my internship in the Human Resources Department at the Bank of Bhutan (BOB). The system uses the 2025 Training Dataset to demonstrate how machine learning can assist HR professionals in predicting training costs and recommending suitable training programs based on employee profiles.

Disclaimer: This system is a prototype developed solely for testing and demonstration purposes. It is not intended as an official tool for operational use.
